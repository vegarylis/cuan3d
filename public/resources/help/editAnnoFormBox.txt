Here you can change the position and the size of the selected annotation box.

The box can be moved around with the left mouse button. Alternatively you can use the menu on the left to change the position of the box.

The size of the box can be changed on the left side menu as well.

By pressing crtl+z on your keyboard manipulations can be undone.
Clicking on the button 'Delete annotation' will delete the whole annotation.
