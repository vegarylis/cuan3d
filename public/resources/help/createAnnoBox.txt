Here you can create a new annotation in the form of a box. 

Clicking on a point on the cuneiform tablet positions the box in the scene. 

The box can be moved around with the left mouse button. Alternatively you can use the menu on the left to change the position of the box.

The size of the box can be changed on the left side menu as well.

'Reset' deletes your annotation and takes you back to the main menu.
